The band Veget

I've founded this band in March 2015 along with several musically talented buddies.
We have organized and performed at a variety of celebrations and concerts. We also participate in regional events in Záhorie.
Our repertoire consists of Czech and Slovak traditional music, the most popular hits of Czechoslovakia and the world.


Members of the band:
Milan Cák - saxophone, keyboard, vocals
Barbora Šajdíková - guitar, voice
Dominik Nemečkay - saxophone, bassguitar
Róbert Drinka - saxophone, voice
Ondrej Flimel - drums

The big band Storm

The big band was founded in 2008 by music school students from Šaštín-Stráže.
Currently there are more than 20 members.
We participate in various competitions. We also participate in regional events in Záhorie.
Our repertoire consists of Czech and Slovak traditional music and the world's most popular and jazz hits.
